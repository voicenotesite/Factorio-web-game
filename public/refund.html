EU Consumer Right of Withdrawal (14 days)

Under Directive 2011/83/EU and the Polish Ustawa o prawach konsumenta (Journal of Laws 2014, item 827), EU consumers have the right to withdraw from a purchase within 14 days without giving any reason.

Exception for digital content (Art. 16(m) of the Directive): If the Digital Content is supplied fully before the 14-day period ends and the consumer expressly agreed to early supply and acknowledged the loss of the withdrawal right, the right of withdrawal is lost. By clicking "Buy" or "Subscribe", you:

  • Expressly agree that the Digital Content will be delivered immediately,
  • Acknowledge that you lose your right of withdrawal once the Digital Content has been fully delivered,
  • Confirm that you understand this exception under EU consumer law.
