Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-19

Data Controller

The data controller is the Service operator (contact below). The Service is hosted and operated from Poland and complies with the Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR / RODO).

Data We Collect

  • Account data — username, synthetic email (username@novactorio.io), password hash. Stored by Supabase (auth.provider).
  • Game data — game progress, save files, inventory, building placements, chat messages, friend lists. Stored in Supabase PostgreSQL.
  • Payment data — none. All payments are processed and stored by Paddle.com, which acts as an independent data controller. We never receive or store payment card details.
  • Technical data — we may collect basic usage analytics (page load, game version) for performance monitoring. No IP addresses are logged persistently.

Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — account creation, game saves, friend lists, purchases.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — anti-cheat enforcement, service security, community moderation.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — optional cookies and analytics if implemented in the future.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with essential sub-processors: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth — GDPR-compliant, DPA in place) and Paddle.com (payment processing — independent controller).

Data Retention

Account data is retained until the User requests deletion. Game saves are retained in cloud storage (Supabase) during active account status. Upon account deletion, all associated data is erased within 30 days.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to: access, rectify, erase (right to be forgotten), restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required for authentication (Supabase Auth). No tracking, advertising or third-party cookies are used.
