Trade Forum — the player-to-player marketplace within Novactorio. Service Fee — a fixed 5% fee charged to the seller for listing and completing a trade through the platform. Off-Network Transaction — any trade agreed upon via the Forum but finalized outside of the Novactorio system (e.g. direct payments, external wallets). The Provider charges a service fee of 5% (pięć procent) of the agreed transaction value. This is a fee for using the Trade Forum infrastructure (listing, matchmaking, verified chat, dispute logs) — not a commission on the transaction. The fee is deducted from the seller's proceeds at the time the trade is marked as completed within the Forum system. The fee is non-refundable once the trade has been executed.

Chat logs are retained for a period of 12 months and are accessible only to the Provider for fraud prevention and fee verification purposes.
